The System

Child Labour monitoring and remediation system (CLMS) - Ghana is :

An ACTIVE process for reducing and/or stopping child labour

Involves consistent direct OBSERVATIONS to identify child labourers/children at risk

Refers child labourers and children at risk to satisfactory and sustainable ALTERNATIVES

Ensures withdrawn children are NOT replaced with other children

Informs NATIONAL POLICY on child labour related activities at all levels

Implementation and MONITORING of child labour related conventions and laws at both national and international levels
